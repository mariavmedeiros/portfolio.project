About Maria

A brazilian Frontend Developer, based in USA

Born and raised in a small town of the state of Sao Paulo in Brazil, I moved to United States in April 2017.
Since 9 years old, I've been working as a nanny in Brazil. In 2012 I went to College, and I studied for 4 years Mechatronics Engineering. At college, I had a chance to take a few classes of programming languages, and I loved it but I never had a chance to learn more.
In 2017 I came to USA as an au pair, I had a chance to meet an amazing family that hosted me for 2 years, and my husband. In March of 2022 I decided to go deep into learning more about coding. I found SheCodes, coding workshops for women. I did all the workshops that they have it.

  • -SheCodes Basics; I learnt HTML, CSS, JavaScript and VS code.
  • - SheCodes Plus; Advanced JavaScrip, API,GitHub, Bootstrap, and hosting.
  • - SheCodes Responsive; FlexBox, SEO, and Responsive.
  • -SheCodes React;I could use all the skills that I learnt before but now with React.

My passions outside of work include weightlifting, running, yoga, going out to restaurants and eat good food. I love to spend time with husband and our dog, we always go to a walk at the beach, hike and roadtrips.
